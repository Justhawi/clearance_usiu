How It Works

Submit Request

Submit your clearance request through the online system. All relevant departments will be notified automatically.

Department Review

Each department reviews your request and updates the status in real-time. You'll receive notifications as updates occur.

Get Cleared

Once all departments approve your request, you'll receive your clearance certificate and can proceed with graduation.
