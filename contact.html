Frequently Asked Questions

How long does clearance take?

Typically, clearance takes 3-5 business days. However, this depends on each department's review process and whether any issues need to be resolved.

What if my clearance is rejected?

If a department rejects your clearance, you'll receive a notification with the reason. You can address the issue and resubmit your request.

Can I track my clearance status?

Yes! Once you log in to your student dashboard, you can see real-time updates from all departments regarding your clearance status.

What documents do I need?

Generally, you don't need to upload documents. Each department will verify your status internally. However, if requested, you may need to provide additional documentation.
